Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your purchase. If you're not completely satisfied, we're here to help with easy returns and exchanges.

30 Days

Return window from delivery date

With Tags

Items must be unworn with original tags

Free Returns

We cover return shipping costs

How to Return

1

Log into Your Account

Go to your account dashboard and find the order you want to return.

2

Start Return Process

Click "Return Item" and select the items you want to return along with the reason.

3

Print Return Label

We'll email you a prepaid return shipping label. Print it and attach to your package.

4

Ship Your Return

Drop off your package at any authorized shipping location or schedule a pickup.

5

Receive Refund

Once we receive and process your return, you'll get your refund within 3-5 business days.

How to Exchange

1

Choose Exchange Option

During the return process, select "Exchange" instead of "Return" and choose your new size or color.

2

Return Original Item

Follow the same return process to send back your original item.

3

Receive New Item

We'll ship your exchange item as soon as we process your return. You'll get tracking information via email.

Exchange Policy

  • • Exchanges are free of charge
  • • Available for size and color changes
  • • Must be within 30 days of delivery
  • • Original item must be in new condition

Return Conditions

Items We Accept

  • • Unworn items with original tags attached
  • • Items in original packaging
  • • Items returned within 30 days
  • • Items that haven't been altered or damaged
  • • Items purchased directly from JOOG

Items We Cannot Accept

  • • Worn or used items
  • • Items without original tags
  • • Items returned after 30 days
  • • Items that have been altered or damaged
  • • Items purchased from third-party sellers

Refund Information

Processing Time

3-5 business days after we receive your return

Refund Method

Refunded to your original payment method

Shipping Costs

Original shipping costs are non-refundable

Need Help with Your Return?

Our customer service team is here to assist you with any return or exchange questions.
