How to Measure

Use a Measuring Tape

Use a flexible measuring tape for accurate measurements. Keep it snug but not tight.

Measure Over Underwear

Take measurements over your undergarments for the most accurate fit.

Stand Naturally

Stand in a natural, relaxed posture when taking measurements.

Sizing Tips

General Tips

  • • Measure yourself regularly as sizes can change
  • • Different brands may have slightly different sizing
  • • When in doubt, size up rather than down
  • • Consider the fabric - some materials stretch more
  • • Check product descriptions for fit notes

Fit Guide

  • • Snug: Fits close to the body
  • • Regular: Standard fit with some ease
  • • Relaxed: Loose, comfortable fit
  • • Oversized: Intentionally loose fit
  • • Fitted: Tailored, close-fitting
